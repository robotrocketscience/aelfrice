# Code of Conduct

## TL;DR

Be respectful. Focus on the work. Disagree about ideas, not about people.

## Pledge

Everyone participating in the aelfrice project, in any forum (issues, PRs, discussions, security reports, the eventual community channels), commits to making participation a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

Behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.
- Showing empathy towards other community members.

Behaviour that is unacceptable:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information (physical or electronic) without explicit permission.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces (the GitHub repository, issues, PRs, discussions, security reports, and any community channels) and when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project owner at **conduct@robotrocketscience.com**. Reports will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response appropriate to the circumstances.

The project owner is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident. Further details of specific enforcement may be posted separately.
